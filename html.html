Morlvision is a song contest for weirdos. Hosted live on Twitch by known comedian Sean Morley and supported by the reanimated corpse of Terry Wogan.

Contestants have a month to make a song and video lasting no longer than 2m30s. All songs are then broadcast during the show, there's a public vote and a winner is declared.
